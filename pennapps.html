I found my way onto the design team at PennApps in the spring of 2015. Since then, I've worked with countless number of people to bring PennApps to life. From countless design meetings to last minute banner orders, being a part of the PennApps team has been a defining experience in my college career.

Mascot Design

I've been involved with PennApps Design since the spring of 2015. My first big contribution to the team was creating the Platypus mascot, and since then, I've been in charge of creating the different iterations for each event.

By titling the fall 2016 event “Hacking for the Hack of It,” our goal was to return the emphasis of hacking back to the basics — learning, collaborating, and having fun.
