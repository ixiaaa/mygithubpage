PennApps
  • PennApps began in 2009 as the first student-run hackathon.
  • In 2015, it became the largest college hackathon with its 12th iteration hosting over 2000 people from various countries and offering over $60k in prizes.
  • PennApps aims to make the hackathon accessible for everyone by providing support from complete travel reimbursements to hacking mentors available all weekend.
  • Still going strong with its 16th iteration in the Fall of 2017!
  • For more information, visit www.pennapps.com.
