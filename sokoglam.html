GRAPHIC DESIGN INTERN

Soko Glam

May - August 2017
For the summer of 2017, I worked as the graphic design intern at Soko Glam, an online marketplace for Korean beauty. I created marketing materials, including printed materials, website ads and banners, and newsletters, in addition to assisting with art direction for various projects. I was also able to develop my skills in UI/UX and web design. As one of the two designers on the Soko Glam team, I was able to gain design and creative experience through a variety of different projects.

Etude House Marketing Campaign: My Lips But Bolder

One of my projects at Soko Glam was the marketing campaign for Etude House's Dear Darling Water Gel Tints. I worked on art direction and graphic design, bringing to life the idea that fresh, bold lips can give you that extra boost of confidence.
